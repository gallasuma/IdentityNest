Choosing the Right Tools: A Framework for Assessing IAM, IGA, and PIM/PAM Solutions

Cultivate a robust cybersecurity strategy by choosing the right Identity and Access Management (IAM), Identity Governance and Administration (IGA), and Privileged Identity Management/Privileged Access Management (PIM/PAM) solutions. Ensure seamless digital operations, enhanced compliance, and fortified data protection with our guidance.

Identity and Access Management

IAM ensures secure and efficient access to technology resources, streamlining user authentication and authorization. Think of IAM as the friendly gatekeeper of the digital world, making sure the right people can breeze through to their online tools with ease.

Identity Governance and Administration

IGA extends this by managing and auditing identity and access policies, enhancing compliance and security. IGA is like the wise overseer, keeping an eye on who's doing what, making sure everything's above board.

Privileged Identity Management/Privileged Access Management

PIM/PAM focuses on controlling and monitoring access to critical systems by privileged users, protecting sensitive data and infrastructure. PIM/PAM - the elite guard for your most secret digital treasures, ensuring only the most trusted hands have the keys.
ForgeRock & PingFederate

IAM

ForgeRockIDM is a modern identity management solution that allows organizations to accelerate secure workforce access, increase operational efficiencies, and mitigate security risks. ForgeRock IDM provides comprehensive lifecycle management capabilities for any identity - employee, contractor, and things.

PingFederate is an enterprise federation server that enables user authentication and single sign-on. It serves as a global authentication authority that allows customers, employees, and partners to securely access all the applications they need from any device.

SailPoint & Saviynt

IGA

The SailPoint Identity Security Cloud Platform is purpose-built for today’s enterprise demands, delivering an intelligent, autonomous identity foundation that securely fuels your business.

Saviynt enables organizations to merge divergent identity, role, and group definitions across their on-premise, hybrid, and cloud infrastructures to create a single, authoritative identity source. The platform natively integrates with business-critical IaaS and SaaS products, and organizations can limit access beyond the coarse-grained application level and drill down to the “edit/read” level using fine-grained access entitlements.

BeyondTrust & CyberArk

PIM/PAM

BeyondTrust Privileged Access Management is designed to be a flexible solution that allows your organization to secure and manage its unique privilege ecosystem—and solve the security concerns that are front-of-mind.

CyberArk Privileged Access Management solutions address a wide range of use cases to secure privileged credentials and secrets wherever they exist: on-premises, in the cloud, and anywhere in between.
